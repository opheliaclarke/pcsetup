3 Under the desk  — how it sits

On a wheeled pedestal, with the desk's minimum height locked so it can never come down onto the case.

THE ONE RULE Never a radiator under the 6000

The floor and the above-PSU shelf (⑤ and ⑦ on the build side) are fresh-air fans only. A radiator there would pre-heat the 6000's intake and cost it 7–9 °C — the difference between "runs cool" and "throttles." Radiators go on the front (④) and top (①); fresh air goes under the GPU.

Diagrams are schematic — they show placement and airflow, not exact millimetres. The V3000 Plus ships with no fans, so you supply the floor, above-PSU and rear fans (~7–8× 140 mm); the two radiators bring their own.
